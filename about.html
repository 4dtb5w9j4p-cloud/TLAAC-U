About The Legal Advisory Aid Camp

The Legal Advisory Aid Camp (TLAAC) provides free legal aid, community legal education, and research on Uganda’s legal framework. Our mission is to increase access to justice through practical assistance and evidence-based policy work.

Our approach

  • Free community legal clinics
  • Short, accessible research briefs
  • Workshops and community outreach
  • Partnerships with local organisations
